Nana Ama McBrown

Nana Ama McBrown,born Felicity Ama Agyemang, is a renowned Ghanaian actress, TV presenter, and music writer.
She has garnered acclaim for her performances in Ghanaian films, earning multiple awards for her versatile acting skills.
Notably, she has been the esteemed cooking show"McBrown's Kitchen," showcasing her culinary skills and engaging in captivating conversations with celebrity guests.
Her infectious personality, coupled with her philanthropic efforts and advocacy work, has solidified her as a beloved public figure in Ghana.
McBrown's influence extends beyond entertainment, as she continues to inspire and uplift others through her multifaceted talents and impactful contibutions to society.

Victoria Orenze

Victoria Orenze is a Nigerian gospel music minister known for her powerful and soul stirring performances.
Her songs marked by heartfelt worship and deep spirituality, have gained immense popularity across Nigeria and beyond.
Victoria is celebrated for her unique ability to lead listeners into moments of profound worship, evoking a strong sense of spiritual connection.
Through her music, she continous to inspire and uplift countless individuals with message of faith,love and hope


Pastor Chris

Pastor Chris Oyakhilome, also known as Pastor Chris, is a prominent Nigerian Pastor, author, and the founder of Believers' Loveworld Incorporated, also known as Christ Embassy.
As a dynamic preacher and teacher of God's Word, Pastor Chris has reached millions worldwide through his television ministry, healing and miracle crusades, and various global events.
He is recognized for his impactful teachings on faith, healing and the power of the Holy Spirit, inspiring countless individuals to embrace their spiritual journeys in Christ.
Pastor Chris's Ministry has had profound influence, shaping the lives and faith of many across different continents.
